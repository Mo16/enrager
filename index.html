Frequently asked questions

What is EngageR?

EngageR is a platform that boosts Twitter engagement and brand awareness in the crypto and NFT space.

How does EngageR work?

EngageR allows partners to set missions for engagers, rewarding them for interactions on Twitter with just one or two clicks. Creators can;

  • Create any mission type (retweet, comment, hashtags and more)
  • Integrate rewards system
  • Analyse data to measure brand awareness

Is my data and information secure with EngageR?

Absolutely! We prioritize the security of your data (view-only access), and ensure robust protection of our API.

What are the pricing options for EngageR?

Discover our tailored packages by reaching out to us. EngageR offers affordable prices, catering to brands of all sizes.

How do I become a partner?

To become a partner, simply sign up on our website and go through our partner onboarding process. Once approved, you can start setting missions and rewarding engagers.
